The Revolving Internet

Created in 2010 (therevolvinginternet.com) A browser-based artwork where the entire Google homepage slowly rotates 360°. The page remains functional – you can still type, click, search.

wikipedia.org/wiki/Personal_Reflections

Personal reflections

The rotation creates cognitive disorientation, breaking predictive processing patterns. It interrupts our habitual eye movements and attention, thus highlighting how much our sense of ‘orientation’ depends on digital stability.
